..:: The Power of Music ::..
..:: Settlement ::..
As we have described in the Intro tab, Chapter 20 - The Power of Music introduces the last group of Ascended Goods:

  • Precious Ascended Goods , produced inside Elixir, Magic Dust and Gems Manufactories, respectively. Similarly to other Ascended Goods, they require Unurium and Standard Goods for production.

..:: Buildings ::..

The settlement of this chapter consists of the following buildings:

  • Harmony Grove - the portal of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Main Hall via a regular street. Stores the settlement resources and offers production of Bars , (the only capped resource of this chapter) and Songs (required to progress in the Tech Tree).

    Harmony Grove - Level 1
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Harmony Grove at level 1 can only produce Bars. Upgrade the building to at least level 2 to start producing Songs.

    Harmony Grove - Level 2

    Level 2 unlocks the following productions:
    • Songs - you can choose from 3 different productions (each requires a different combination of instruments)
    Note that the productions do not include the Blooming Trader Guild wonder, so your outputs might be higher depending on its level (however, it does not increase the production of Songs).

    Harmony Grove - Level 3

    Harmony Grove - Level 4
    ..:: Productions ::..

    At level 4, the final production of Songs is unlocked:
    • Enar's Blues - the most efficient production of Songs; requires all 3 instruments

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    You need to build additional Ensembles (3 from each) in order to upgrade the Harmony Grove from level 3 to level 4. You will end up with 6 Ensembles from each in total at this point.
  • Instrument Makers - main production buildings of this settlement. Cannot be upgraded any further. Need to be directly connected with Manufactories (Violin Maker with Refined Manufactory, Flute Maker with Precious Manufactory and Drum Maker with Basic Manufactory). Require Bars and Sentient Goods to produce Plucked Violin, Horn Flutes and Barrel Drums, respectively.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
  • Ensembles - need to be connected (directly or indirectly) with the Harmony Grove. Cannot be upgraded any further. They provide Melody, Accompaniment and Rhythm, the requirements needed to upgrade Harmony Grove to higher levels. Every upgrade of the Harmony Grove consumes the needed requirements, so you need 6 of each in total. That makes 18 Ensembles required in total (6+6+6) to upgrade Harmony Grove to level 4.
    What's more, each Ensemble increases the production of Bars inside the Harmony Grove.

..:: Tips & Tricks ::..

  • The usual amount of Instrument Makers players build in this chapter is 4 pieces from each. That amount provides enough instruments to let your Harmony Grove constantly produce Songs.
  • The Vortex of Storage proves to be an efficient Ancient Wonder in this chapter. If you upgrade this wonder to at least level 14, you can finish this chapter with Harmony Grove at level 3 (as your Bars storage capacity will be large enough to finish the last and most demanding technologies of the research tree). This will reduce the amount of necessary Ensembles from 18 to 9 and so reduce the occupied space and lower the amount of required resources. On the other hand, keep in mind that this approach will not allow you to finish the entire questline.
  • Many players manage to complete this chapter quite fast. Be prepared to face high requirements of Ascended Goods and to juggle with all 3 Tiers of Ascended Goods at the same time.
