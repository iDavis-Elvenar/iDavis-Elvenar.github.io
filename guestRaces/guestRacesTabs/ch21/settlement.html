..:: The Vallorian Legend ::..
..:: Settlement ::..
As we have described in the Intro tab, Chapter 21 - The Vallorian Legend introduces 2 significant news for the game:

  • Prosperity , a brand-new requirement provided by the core city buildings. These are Main Hall, Residences, Workshops and Barracks. You receive prosperity after upgrading these buildings to the levels unlocked by this chapter. The Prosperity will be required to build the settlements of all guest races from this chapter on.
  • Community Work , a discovered resource, similarly to Orcs, Mana, Seeds and Unurium. It is provided by Residential buildings of your city - they now offer a switchable production system and you can decide for each Residence, whether they pay taxes or offer Community Work! Community Work will be required in various places of the game, such as Research and future buildings' upgrades.

..:: Buildings ::..

The settlement of this chapter consists of the following buildings:

  • Vallorian Dragon Tower - the portal of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Main Hall via a regular street. Stores the settlement resources and offers production of Oblations from level 2, including the Noble Oblations from level 3.

    Vallorian Dragon Tower - Level 1
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Vallorian Dragon Tower at level 1 can only store your resources. You need to upgrade it to level 2 in order to start producing Oblations.

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    Except for Orcs, the Vallorian Dragon Tower also requires Prosperity. Make sure you have upgraded some of the core city buildings before placing the first Settlement buildings.
    Vallorian Dragon Tower - Level 2
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 2 unlocks the following productions:
    • Rude Oblations - require Mana, Lizard Shells and Broken Bones
    • Messy Oblations - require Seeds, Dragon Glass and Destroyed Furniture
    • Dangerous Oblations - require Unurium, Sea Serpent Scales and Splintered Glass
    Note that the productions do not include the Blooming Trader Guild wonder, so your outputs might be higher depending on its level.
    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Vallorian Dragon Tower - Level 3
    ..:: Important ::..
    You need to unlock an additional technology in order to upgrade your Vallorian Dragon Tower to level 3.

    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 3 unlocks the following productions:
    • Noble Oblations - require Community Work, Prey and Orcish Art
    Note that the productions do not include the Blooming Trader Guild wonder, so your outputs might be higher depending on its level.
    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Vallorian Dragon Tower - Level 4
    ..:: Productions ::..

    At level 4, no new productions are added, but the current ones give higher production outputs.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
  • Prey Trail - streets required to connect Vallorian Dragon Tower with the production buildings of this settlement. These are quite expensive for supplies, so make sure you have a steady amount prepared.
  • Vallorian Workshop - production building of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Vallorian Dragon Tower via Prey Trail. Produces Prey (the only capped resource of this chapter, which can be also obtained from Portal Profit instants), Sea Serpent Scales, Lizard Shells and Dragon Glass. Requires Community Work to produce the last 3 resources.
