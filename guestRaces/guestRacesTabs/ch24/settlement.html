..:: Fate of the Bastet ::..
..:: Settlement ::..
As we have described in the Intro tab, Chapter 24 - Fate of the Bastet introduces Nox, a brand-new discovered resource produced in Trader. Nox will be required to construct the production buildings of this chapter. We will describe all details of new buildings in this overview.

..:: Buildings ::..

The settlement of this chapter consists of the following buildings:

  • Temple of Her Divine Purriness - one of the two portals of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Main Hall via a regular street. Stores the settlement resources and offers various production right from level 1.

  • Production buildings - while each of the following buildings belongs to multiple sets, it behaves in each differently. All 4 production buildings help to increase the productions of portals. However, Servants Training Center and Warriors Training Center themselves only take benefit when connected to Temple of Her Divine Purriness. Similarly, Farmers Training Center and Workers Training Center themselves only take benefit when connected to the Laboratory of Magical Sciences. This means some buildings might occupy a spot that's less than idea for them - but perfect for the settlement as a whole. See the table below for further details.
    Servants Training Center Warriors Training Center Farmers Training Center Workers Training Center
    4x6 8x3 3x8 6x4

..:: Tips & Tricks ::..

  • How to prepare for this chapter:
    • You will need a large amount of Nox in this chapter. Its production at the Trader depends on the required Prosperity in your city. To increase your Nox production, you can prepare in advance by constructing earlier Portals that require Prosperity (specifically the Portals from Chapters 21, 22, and 23, with those from Chapters 21 and 22 being very easy to rebuild).
    • A significant amount of Ascended Goods is required to progress through the research tree. Make sure to switch some of your Residential Buildings to produce Community Work. If you have the Maman Le Pré's Antique Shop Ancient Wonder, this Community Work production will also generate Ascended Goods. You can further increase this production by using Ensorcelled Endowment spells on as many cultural buildings as possible and receiving Neighbourly Help. This approach allows you to avoid building additional Manufactories for Ascended Goods.
    • Use Ensorcelled Endowment spells on cultural buildings to also increase the production of Canned Goods in your Workshops.
