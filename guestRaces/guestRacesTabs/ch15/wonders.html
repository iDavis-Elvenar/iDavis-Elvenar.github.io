..:: Wonders ::..
The Chapter 15 introduces two new wonders: Vortex of Storage and Thermal Spring of Youth. In this overview, we will take a look at their effects, avatars and I will share with you a couple of tips and tricks on how to get the most out of these wonders and some of my opinions about their future in-game prognosis.

..:: Vortex of Storage ::..
Size
 3x6
Effects
 Enhances your capped Settlement Goods storage capacity
from 6.5% at level 1 to 55% at level 35
Increases the production boosts for your Sentient Goods
from 120% of basic production at level 1 to 190% of basic production at level 35
Tips & Evaluation
 In Chapter 14, we previously encountered the Sentient Goods production boost in the D111-a "Timewarp" wonder. Its production boost exceeded that of the Vortex of Storage, and as I regularly collect additional Sentient Goods from Orcs productions (thanks to the Pyramid of Purification from Chapter 13), I found no need for an additional boost.
As for the first effect of the Vortex of Storage, increasing the storage capacity of Settlement Portals is a welcome addition, particularly for those with limited Portal Profit instants. Having a higher capacity enhances the efficiency of each Portal Profit used. However, if you actively participate in the weekly Spire of Eternity or events, chances are you already possess dozens of these instants in your inventory. In such a case, using more of them to achieve the desired effect won't be a significant concern, nor the need to construct an additional wonder to your city.
Rating
 6/10
Avatar
..:: Thermal Spring of Youth ::..
Size
 7x3
Effects
 Increases the Population provided by Residences
from 5% at level 1 to 22% at level 35
Adds additional Culture for every Ancient Wonder level present in the city
from 22.5x at level 1 to 165x at level 35
Tips & Evaluation
 Every wonder that offers Population should be considered a must-have structure in your city. The significance of this wonder's effect improves with each new chapter, as the Population provided in Residential buildings increases with each advancement. It is even possible that in the future, the developers may take into account players having this wonder and set the further requirements accordingly. As such, I highly recommend everyone to construct this wonder.
The effect gets even more prominent if you own Premium Residential buildings (for example if you won them from the Spire of Eternity or bought them with Diamonds).
The second effect serves as a valuable addition to the already important Population boost provided by the effect number one.
Rating
 10/10
