..:: Harbours of the Deep ::..
..:: Settlement ::..
As we have described in the Intro tab, Chapter 22 - Harbours of the Deep introduces a new resource to the game:

  • Canned Goods , a brand-new Discovered Resource produced inside Workshops - the Toolbox production (24h) is replaced by the production of Canned Goods. The production time of Canned Goods is 9 hours and they decay, left unused. Similarly to Coins, Supplies and Community Work, the Canned Goods receive cultural bonus.

..:: Buildings ::..

The settlement of this chapter consists of the following buildings:

  • Whale Bay - the portal of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Main Hall via a regular street. Stores the settlement resources and offers various production from level 2.
    Level 1 Level 2 Level 3 Level 4
    8x8 8x8 8x8 8x8
    Whale Bay - Level 1
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Whale Bay at level 1 can only store your resources. You need to upgrade it to level 2 in order to start producing Deep Sea Snails, Lure Weed, Garden Eels and Sea Sponges.

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    Except for Mana and Community Work, the Whale Bay also requires Prosperity. Make sure you have upgraded some of the core city buildings before placing the first Settlement buildings.
    Whale Bay - Level 2
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 2 unlocks the following productions:
    • Deep Sea Tour - requires Canned Goods, Cave Salt and Luminous Shrimps; produces Deep Sea Snails
    • Hidden Cave Tour - requires Canned Goods, Sea Food and Luminous Shrimps; produces Lure Weed
    • Sunset Reef Tour - requires Canned Goods, Shore Fish and Luminous Shrimps; produces Garden Eels
    • Faraway Lagoon Tour - requires Canned Goods, Kelp Fruits and Luminous Shrimps; produces Sea Sponges
    Note that the productions do not include the Blooming Trader Guild wonder, so your outputs might be higher depending on its level.
    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Whale Bay - Level 3
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 3 unlocks the 4th production slot.
    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Whale Bay - Level 4
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 4 unlocks the 5th production slot.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
  • Provisional Path - streets required to connect Whale Bay with the production buildings of this settlement. These are quite expensive for coins and ascended goods, so make sure you have a steady amount prepared.
  • Fossil Canyon - production building of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Whale Bay via Provisional Path. Produces Sea Food and just like any other production building of this chapter, provides an option to produce Luminious Shrimps (the only capped resource of this chapter, which can be also obtained from Portal Profit instants). Requires Mana to produce Sea Food.
