..:: Elementals ::..
..:: Wonders ::..
The Chapter 12 introduces two new wonders: Sunset Towers and Victory Springs. In this overview, we will take a look at their effects, avatars and I will share with you a couple of tips and tricks on how to get the most out of these wonders and some of my opinions about their future in-game prognosis.

..:: Sunset Towers ::..
Size
 7x3
Effects
 Gives you Divine Seeds every time you open a Neighborly Help Chest. The actual amount of Divine Seeds is calculated as the bonus multiplied by the level of your Main Hall
from 18x at level 1 to 138x at level 35
Reduces the overnight Divine Seed Decay
from 1.2% of decay at level 1 to 8% of decay at level 30
Tips & Evaluation
 The Sunset Towers is a must-have wonder once you discover Seeds. It provides an effective decay reduction that greatly aids in stockpiling this resource for future use. Seeds belong to the "Discovered Resources" category of goods in the game, and as we already know, Discovered Resources will be continually required from the moment they are unlocked.
It undoubtedly makes sense to construct this wonder and upgrade it to level 30 (which offers the maximum reduction) as soon as possible. Additionally, the effect that grants you Seeds when opening Neighbourly Help Chests can become even more prominent when combined with the Scout's Tavern ancient wonder in Chapter 20.
Rating
 9/10

..:: Victory Springs ::..
Size
 6x4
Effects
 Increases your Training Grounds Training Speed
from 15% at level 1 to 112% at level 35
Gives your Light Melee Units increased damage
from 106% of basic damage at level 1 to 140% of basic damage at level 30
Tips & Evaluation
 I highly recommend building all three wonders that decrease the training time in Military Buildings: Needles of the Tempest for Barracks, Victory Springs for Training Grounds, and Flying Academy for Mercenary Camp. I personally prioritize the Barracks and Mercenary Camp military buildings more, as I find the use of units from these buildings more crucial in tournaments than those provided by the Training Grounds (and also due to the fact that two out of the five units provided by the Training Grounds require Orcs, and a reduction in their training time would only increase the demand for orcs (which not everyone can afford)). So, if you are deciding between these three wonders and which one to build first, I recommend starting with either of the other two first. I utilize the Lightranged units and Mages from Barracks and Mercenary Camp much more than Dryads or Ghastly Banshees from Training Grounds.
Moreover, the second effect of the Victory Springs wonder is highly significant and should undoubtedly find a place in your city at some point.

 8/10
