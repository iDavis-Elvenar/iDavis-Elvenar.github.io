..:: What's new in this chapter? ::..
Divine Protection
Chapter 23 will unlock Level 5 for some of your units. Units at Level 5 are known as "Divine Units." They gain a new ability called Divine Protection, which blocks debuffs from unit types they are strong against.

You can view this ability in the unit window:

Flashbacks to the past...
Chapter 23 offers a break from discovering new resources and features. Instead, the story will guide you through the highlights of past chapters. Alongside brand-new, beautiful buildings, you'll also need to construct settlements for some of the previous Guest Races, such as the Dwarves, Fairies, Dragons, and Halflings.
