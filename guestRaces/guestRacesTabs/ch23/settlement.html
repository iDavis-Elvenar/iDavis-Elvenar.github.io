..:: Secrets of the Unicorn ::..
..:: Settlement ::..
As we have described in the Intro tab, Chapter 23 - Secrets of the Unicorn doesn't introduce any new special resource to the game, but will require you to build some of the previous settlements. We will describe all details of new buildings in this overview.

..:: Buildings ::..

The settlement of this chapter consists of the following buildings:

  • Enar's Sanctum - the portal of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Main Hall via a regular street. Stores the settlement resources and offers various production right from level 1.
    Level 1 Level 2 Level 3 Level 4
    6x6 7x7 8x8 9x9
    Enar's Sanctum - Level 1
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Enar's Sanctum produces Unicorn Blossoms. It offers 3 different production options: 15m, 1h and 3h. Each requires Coins and Supplies.

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    Except for Prosperity (which you can get by upgrading some of the core city buildings), the Enar's Sanctum also requires resources that you can receive from Dwarven, Fairy, Orc and Halfling settlement. Construct the settlement buildings of these guest races to start producing these resources.
    Enar's Sanctum - Level 2
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 2 unlocks 3rd production slot. Enar's Sanctum produces Unicorn Blossoms. It offers 3 different production options: 15m, 1h and 3h. Each requires Coins and Supplies.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    To upgrade the Enar's Sanctum to level 2, your portal buildings will have to reach the following levels in order to have the capacity required for upgrade: Dwarven Portal level 4, Fairy Portal level 4, Orc Portal level 3 and Halflings Portal level 1.
    Enar's Sanctum - Level 3
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 3 unlocks 4th production slot. Enar's Sanctum produces Unicorn Blossoms. It offers 3 different production options: 15m, 1h and 3h. Each requires Coins and Supplies.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    To upgrade the Enar's Sanctum to level 3, your portal buildings will have to reach the following levels in order to have the capacity required for upgrade: Dwarven Portal level 4, Fairy Portal level 4, Orc Portal level 4 and Halflings Portal level 1.
    Enar's Sanctum - Level 4
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 4 unlocks 5th production slot. Enar's Sanctum produces Unicorn Blossoms. It offers 3 different production options: 15m, 1h and 3h. Each requires Coins and Supplies.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    To upgrade the Enar's Sanctum to level 4, your portal buildings will have to reach the following levels in order to have the capacity required for upgrade: Dwarven Portal level 4, Fairy Portal level 4, Orc Portal level 4 and Halflings Portal level 4.
  • Production buildings - each of the following guest races - Dwarves, Fairies, Orcs, and Halflings - has one new production building introduced in this chapter. These new production buildings must be connected to their respective portals using the original settlement street (e.g., the Dwarven Heritage Hut must be connected to the Dwarven Portal via Portal Tracks). These buildings use Community Work and Unicorn Blossoms (produced in Enar's Sanctum) to generate Dwarven Spirits, Fairy Spirits, Orc Spirits, and Halfling Spirits, respectively. These spirits, along with various goods and special resources, are then used in other of these production buildings to produce the final resources required for advancement in the research tree. See the table below for further details.
    Dwarven Heritage Hut Fairies' Heritage Hut Orcish Heritage Hut Halflings' Heritage Hut

    Dwarven Heritage Hut
    ..:: Productions ::..


    Dwarven Heritage Hut uses Unicorn Blossoms (from Enar's Sanctum) to produce Dwarven Spirits. Spirits from other Heritage Huts are used to produce Queen Honey, Dragon Teeth and Flower Wreaths.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Fairies' Heritage Hut
    ..:: Productions ::..


    Fairies' Heritage Hut uses Unicorn Blossoms (from Enar's Sanctum) to produce Fairy Spirits. Spirits from other Heritage Huts are used to produce Dragon Teeth, Flower Wreaths and Azurite.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Orcish Heritage Hut
    ..:: Productions ::..


    Orcish Heritage Hut uses Unicorn Blossoms (from Enar's Sanctum) to produce Orc Spirits. Spirits from other Heritage Huts are used to produce Flower Wreaths, Azurite and Queen Honey.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Halflings' Heritage Hut
    ..:: Productions ::..


    Halflings' Heritage Hut uses Unicorn Blossoms (from Enar's Sanctum) to produce Halfling Spirits. Spirits from other Heritage Huts are used to produce Azurite, Queen Honey and Dragon Teeth..

    ..:: Requirements ::..
..:: Tips & Tricks ::..

  • Before you can construct Enar's Sanctum, you must first build the portals for the previous guest races: Dwarves, Fairies, Orcs, and Halflings. (This is required unless you've stockpiled some of their resources in advance; however, you will still need these portals to upgrade Enar's Sanctum to higher levels.) To upgrade Enar's Sanctum to level 4, all four previous portals must be upgraded to level 4.
  • Note that Portal Profit instants do not work for previous portals - they only provide resources for the current chapter, which in Chapter 23 are Unicorn Blossoms.
