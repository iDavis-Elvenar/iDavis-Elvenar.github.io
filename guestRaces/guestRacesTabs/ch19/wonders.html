..:: Revenge of the Exile ::..
..:: Wonders ::..
The Chapter 19 introduces two new wonders: Vallorian Seal Tower and Tree of Enlightenment. In this overview, we will take a look at their effects, avatars and I will share with you a couple of tips and tricks on how to get the most out of these wonders and some of my opinions about their future in-game prognosis.

..:: Vallorian Seal Tower ::..
Size
 5x6
Effects
 Gives a percentage of Unurium for Coins Pickup from Residences
from 0.024% at level 1 to 0.184% at level 35 (grows linearly)
Reduces the decay of Unurium
to 8.8% at level 1 and to 2% at level 30 (grows linearly)
Tips & Evaluation
 In my opinion, the Vallorian Seal Tower is overshadowed by the Temple of Spirits wonder introduced in Chapter 18. Both wonders focus on Unurium, and based on my experiences, having constructed and fully upgraded the Temple of Spirits to its highest level, I have never found the need to produce additional Unurium in the Trader. If you actively participate in the Spire of Eternity (which I highly recommend), the Temple of Spirits will provide you with more than enough Unurium, rendering the construction of the Vallorian Seal Tower unnecessary. In comparison, the Vallorian Seal Tower offers less Unurium per week and consumes more space in your city. However, if you are not actively engaged in the Spire of Eternity or do not regularly collect event currency in the outskirts of your city, the Vallorian Seal Tower can serve as a viable passive source of Unurium, although it may not always suffice without additional production in your Trader (since only one of its effects is specifically aimed at increasing Unurium collection).

..:: Tree of Enlightenment ::..
Size
 4x3
Effects
 Produces an amount of Rangers every 3 hours (as a percentage of your current Units per Squad)
from 22.5% at level 1 to 172.5% at level 35 (grows linearly)
Increases the production boosts for your Ascended Goods
from total increase of basic production to 124% at level 1 and to 284% at level 35 (grows linearly)
Tips & Evaluation
 The effect of increasing the production boost of your Ascended Goods is not as significant as the Ascended Goods decay reduction (which brings the Ancestral Resting Place wonder in Chapter 20) or the possibility to gain Ascended Goods as a bonus when collecting other productions (brought by the Maman Le Pr√©'s Antique Shop in Chapter 21). However, the Tree of Enlightenment becomes the second smallest wonder in the game (with the first one being the Golden Abyss), so taking its size into consideration, I still evaluate this wonder positively and it even found a place in my city.
The rangers production is a nice addition, but not a primary reason why would I build this wonder.
