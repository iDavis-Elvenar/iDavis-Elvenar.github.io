..:: Revenge of the Exile ::..
..:: Settlement ::..
As we have described in the Intro tab, Chapter 19 - Revenge of the Exile introduces the second group of Ascended Goods:

  • Refined Ascended Goods , produced inside Crystal, Scrolls and Silk Manufactories, respectively. Similarly to other Ascended Goods, they require Unurium and Standard Goods for production.

..:: Buildings ::..

The settlement of this chapter consists of the following buildings:

  • Fortress of Revenge - the portal of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Main Hall via a regular street. Stores the settlement resources and offers production of Ultimate Creatures (only at levels 2 and 3). Once the portal is sold at level 4, you receive the Ultimate Matter - the resource required to unlock the final technology.

    • Ultimate Creatures - you can choose from 2 different productions (each requires Unurium + combination of Black Magic and Creatures of the Light or White Magic and Creatures of the Night).

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    To upgrade the Fortress of Revenge to level 4, you need 24x Dark Matter. You can get this resource from selling Tower of Darkness (Tower at level 2). It is the only way to obtain Dark Matter. In order to obtain 24x Dark Matter, you need to build 48x Tower of Light (as upgrading the Tower of Light to Tower of Darkness requires White Matter - resource that can only be obtained from selling the Tower of Light).
    Fortress of Revenge - Level 3
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 3 increases the productions of Ultimate Creatures.

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    You need to sell additional 24 Towers of Darkness in order to upgrade the Fortress of Revenge to level 3. For this you will need to build additional 48 Towers of Light (as upgrading the Tower of Light to Tower of Darkness requires White Matter - resource that can only be obtained from selling the Tower of Light).
    Fortress of Revenge - Level 4
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Fortress of Revenge at level 4 does not offer any production.

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    You need to sell additional 24 Towers of Darkness in order to upgrade the Fortress of Revenge to level 4. For this you will need to build additional 48 Towers of Light (as upgrading the Tower of Light to Tower of Darkness requires White Matter - resource that can only be obtained from selling the Tower of Light).


    The only purpose of Fortress of Revenge at level 4 is to actually sell it. It will grant you the Ultimate Matter - resource required to unlock the final technology of the chapter.
    Note: you don't need to wait for the upgrade to finish in order to receive the Ultimate Matter.
  • Towers - expiring production buildings of White Magic (produced by Tower at level 1 - the Tower of Light) and Black Magic (produced by Tower at level 2 - the Tower of Darkness). Both, The White and Black Magic, are capped resources and are required to produce Creatures of the Light and Creatures of the Night , respectively, inside the Breeding Facilities. Towers are expiring building and only last 7 days. Every time you sell the building, you receive the respective Matter (White Matter from selling Tower of Light and Black Matter from selling Tower of Darkness).

    Tower of Light
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Tower of Light is the main source of White Magic. Once upgraded to level 2, it will turn into Tower of Darkness and start producing Dark Magic.

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    Coins, Orcs and Ascended Goods are required to build the Tower of Light.


    After you sell the Tower of Light, you receive White Matter. This is required to upgrade the Tower of Light to level 2 (Tower of Darkness).
    Tower of Darkness
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Tower of Darkness is the main source of Dark Magic.

    ..:: Requirements ::..

    Coins, Orcs, Ascended Goods and White Matter is required to build the Tower of Darkness. You can receive White Matter from selling the Tower of Light.


    Once the Tower of Darkness is sold, it will grant you Dark Matter - resource required to upgrade the Fortress of Revenge to higher levels.
  • Magical Warpath - streets required to connect Fortress of Revenge with Breeding Facilities. These are quite expensive for Mana, so make sure you have a steady amount prepared.
  • Breeding Facility - needs to be connected via Magical Warpath with the Fortress of Revenge. It is the main source of Creatures of the Light and Creatures of the Night . Further upgrades of this building increase the productions.

..:: Tips & Tricks ::..

  • Both, the White Magic and Dark Magic are the capped resources of this chapter. However, it is not possible to rely on Portal Profit instants only. Except for the White and Dark Magic provided by Towers of Light and Towers of Darkness, they also grant you the White and Dark Matter once you sell them. You will need 72x Dark Matter in total to upgrade the Fortress of Revenge to level 4, so you will need to build at least 72x Tower of Darkness (which means 144x Towers of Light).
  • You don't need to finish upgrades or construction processes in order to receive the resources upon selling. You can sell the Tower of Light right after you start the construction process to receive White Matter. Similarly, you can sell the Tower of Darkness right after you start the upgrading process to receive Dark Matter and you can also sell the Fortress of Revenge right after you start upgrading it from level 3 to receive the Ultimate Matter.
  • Note that some quests want you to produce a specific amount of White and Dark Matter, or to sell some towers. If you aim to only go with the minimal setup required to upgrade the Fortress of Revenge to level 4 (so to build only 72x Towers of Darkness and get the rest of White and Dark Magic with Portal Profit instants), you may want to leave some reserve so that you won't need to build additional towers once such quests arrive. See the Quests tab to learn more about your upcoming quests.
