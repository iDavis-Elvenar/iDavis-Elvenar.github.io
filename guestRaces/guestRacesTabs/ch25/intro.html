..:: The Purpose of Life ::..
..:: Intro ::..

Vallorion appears in the Main Hall, and to everyone's surprise, she seems to have no evil intentions... But can we believe her?

Rather, Enar, the "God of Insignificance, Coercion, and Dependence", is said to have painted a terrible picture of Vallorion. We have been led astray by him. Indeed, Vallorion seems to have an explanation for all the events of the past, which at the very least cast doubt on our previous perspective. What a game are these "gods" playing here with us?

Vallorion argues that people waste their limited time on endless conflict instead of becoming the best version of themselves, and that our most important task in life is to not waste our talents - a potential that, once unfolded, will benefit the community in ways that will overlast our own existence.

To put this into practice, Vallorion proposes establishing dedicated places in the city where her allies, the Orcs, Goblins, Amuni and Dragons can train, learn, and progress through different stages of life with a focus on self-improvement. To help with that, she also introduces the idea of crafting “Finity Gear” in armories, a special equipment meant to help each person push their body and mind further to unlock their full potential.

As the city begins to entertain these ideas, key figures return and old rivalries resurface, creating friction between factions and raising questions about who truly stands closest to Vallorion's favor. With loyalties shifting and tensions building, the city is left with a lingering uncertainty: is this the start of a better path, or the beginning of something far more complicated?
..:: What's new in this chapter? ::..
Finity Gear
Finity Gear is a new Discovered Resource (similarly to Orcs, Mana, Seeds, Unurium, Community Work, Canned Goods and Nox). It is produced inside Armories from level 47. It replaces the 48- and 96- productions of Orcs. For its production you will need Orcs, Unurium and Community Work.

After unlocking the very first technology of this chapter, you will be able to see Finity Gear in the "Discovered Resources" column:


It is the first "fusion" resource introduced in the game. This means that previously discovered resources are used to produce it, so having a good reserve of them will be very helpful.


Similarly to other Discovered resources, Finity Gear will decay every night.
