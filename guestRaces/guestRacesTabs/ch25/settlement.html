..:: The Purpose of Life ::..
..:: Settlement ::..
As we have described in the Intro tab, Chapter 25 - The Purpose of Life introduces Finity Gear, a brand-new discovered resource produced in Armories. Finity Gear will be required to unlock technologies of this chapter and to produce various resources inside the settlement buildings. We will describe all details of new buildings in this overview.

..:: Buildings ::..

The settlement of this chapter consists of the following buildings:

  • Vallorion's Throne - the main building of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Main Hall via a regular street. Stores the settlement resources and offers various production right from level 1. Upgrading it further increases the number of slots in the production queue.
    Level 1 Level 2 Level 3 Level 4
    9x4 9x5 9x6 9x7
    Vallorion's Throne - Level 1
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Vallorion's Throne produces Wisdom of Youth, Wisdom of Adults and Wisdom of Age. For these productions you need various resources including Finity Gear (from Armories) and Experiences (from the production buildings of this chapter).

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Temple of Her Divine Purriness - Level 2
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 2 unlocks 3rd production slot.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Temple of Her Divine Purriness - Level 3
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 3 unlocks 4th production slot.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
    Temple of Her Divine Purriness - Level 4
    ..:: Productions ::..

    Level 4 unlocks 5th production slot.

    ..:: Requirements ::..
  • Road of Potential - streets required to connect Vallorion's Throne with the production buildings of this settlement. These are quite expensive for Nox, so make sure you have a steady amount prepared. One of the cultural buildings of this chapter can help you produce additional Nox.
  • Kids Playground - production building of this settlement. Needs to be connected with Vallorion's Throne via Road of Potential. Produces Growth, Maturity and Childhood Experience, which are later converted into Wisdom of Youth in the Vallorion's Throne. The building expires after 9 days. As you upgrade the building, it becomes smaller. When you sell the building at level 3, you will also receive 1x Wisdom of Youth.

..:: Tips & Tricks ::..

  • How to prepare for this chapter:
    • You will need a large amount of Nox in this chapter. Its production at the Trader depends on the required Prosperity in your city. To increase your Nox production, you can prepare in advance by constructing earlier Portals that require Prosperity (specifically the Portals from Chapters 21 onwards). Serpent Sanctuary cultural building introduced by this chapter may help you to produce additional Nox.
    • Running on short productions inside the settlement production buildings will help you to get the most out of your buildings before they expire.
    • Production of the Vallorion's Throne cannot be increased. However, Generous Guests spell or the Perfect Planning Solutions building will help you increase the production of other buildings of your settlement.
