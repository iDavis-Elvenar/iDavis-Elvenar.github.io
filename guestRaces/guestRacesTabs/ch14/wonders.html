..:: Wonders ::..
The Chapter 14 introduces two new wonders: D111-a "Timewarp" and D222-z "Simia Sapiens". In this overview, we will take a look at their effects, avatars and I will share with you a couple of tips and tricks on how to get the most out of these wonders and some of my opinions about their future in-game prognosis.

..:: D111-a "Timewarp" ::..
Size
 3x6
Effects
 Increases the production boosts for your Sentient Goods
from 124% of basic production at level 1 to 284% of basic production at level 35
Reduces the cooldown of the Tournament rounds
from 12% of reduction at level 1 to 80% of reduction at level 30
Tips & Evaluation
 Chapter 14 stands out as one of the best chapters for wonders, and the D111-a "Timewarp" is a remarkable example. Its unique Tournament cooldown reduction effect allows you to reduce the cooldown by up to 80%. Combining this with the Polar Bear evolving building, which further reduces the cooldown by 20%, results in a total 100% reduction! This enables you to play the entire Tournament, including all rounds, in one session, saving not only time but also valuable resources like Military Boost buildings and Pet Food. With the duration you now spend in Tournament, being covered by the effect of, for instance, the Fire Phoenix, only one Pet Food for it is required and so your success in Tournaments can be significantly enhanced - a strategy widely adopted by veteran players.
You can also utilize the cooldown reduction to your advantage by saving your Military Boost Buildings. Here's what I do: one week, I play the Tournament on Friday, and the following week, I play it on Tuesday. This way, I can cover both Tournaments with just one set of Military Boost buildings, as my Tournament days fall within the 5-day range when these buildings remain active.
In addition, the wonder's second effect is a valuable addition and even surpasses the Sentient Goods production boost offered by the Vortex of Storage wonder in the following chapter. I highly recommend everyone to build this wonder, and I even suggest to accelerate progressing through previous chapters in order to construct it as soon as possible.

 10/10

..:: D222-z "Simia Sapiens" ::..
Size
 6x5
Effects
 Decreases the decay of Sentient Goods
from 1.2% of reduction at level 1 to 8% of reduction at level 30
Gives additional units every time you collect them from your Barracks, Training Grounds or Mercenary Camp
from 106% at level 1 to 146% at level 35
Tips & Evaluation
 The D222-z "Simia Sapiens" wonder is a true gem, as both of its effects are a must-have for your city. The Sentient Goods reduction offers an excelent help with these goods, especially in future chapters where their requirements will notably increase. Upgrading the wonder to its highest level and maximizing this effect will almost negate the need for further wonders' effects on Sentient Goods, such as the one in the Vortex of Storage from Chapter 15.
The second effect is interesting even more, as it increases the output of Units from any Military Buildings without extending the Training time or raising the Training costs. This boost can significantly enhance your city's military power. I highly recommend constructing this wonder as soon as you reach Chapter 14 and prioritize upgrading it to the highest level. The combination of the wonders from this may highly influence your future Tournament's performance.
Rating
 10/10
