..:: Wonders ::..
The Chapter 14 introduces two new wonders: D111-a "Timewarp" and D222-z "Simia Sapiens". In this overview, we will take a look at their effects, avatars and I will share with you a couple of tips and tricks on how to get the most out of these wonders and some of my opinions about their future in-game prognosis.

..:: D111-a "Timewarp" ::..
Size
 4x7
Effects
 Reduces Ascended Goods Decay
to 8.8% at level 1 and to 2% at level 30 (grows linearly)
Produces Ghastly Banshees every 3 hours (as a percentage of your current Units per Squad)
from 22.5% of your squad size at level 1 to 172.5% of your squad size at level 35 (grows linearly)
Tips & Evaluation
 Reduction of decay is one of the most useful benefits for your city and can greatly facilitate the stacking of respective goods. Ascended goods will be an integral part of any future chapter and it makes sense to ease the trading process by reducing the nightly decay. Future wonders may bring additional effects for ascended goods, but at the time of writing this guide, only the Maman Le Pr√©'s Antique Shop is known to give ascended goods when collecting community work, while having almost useless secondary effect. Community Work is highly likely to receive some more interesting wonders in the future, so at this point, I have decided to go with Ancestral Resting Place despite its rather large size.
I do not consider the secondary effect to be a compelling reason for constructing this wonder.
Rating
 8/10

..:: D222-z "Simia Sapiens" ::..
Size
 5x3
Effects
 Reduces the Scouting time
to 94% at level 1 and to 35% at level 35 (grows linearly)
Increases the amount of Neighbourly Help Chests
from 1 additional chest at level 1 to 4 additional chests at level 31 (grows linearly)
Tips & Evaluation
 Before the wonder was introduced, the scouting time reduction was one of the most requested features of the community. Many players now consider the Scout's Tavern to be a highlight of the latest chapters and it becomes a motivation for them to progress forward. This wonder is in my opinion the must-have for everyone who completed the chapter 20.
The second effect can become especially useful if you have other bonuses in your city that grant you additional rewards when giving neighbourly help. This can be the Sunset Towers wonder (granting additional seeds for each collected neighbourly help chest), Crystal Lighthouse / Great Bell Spire wonder (granting additional boosted goods) or Moon Bear pet (providing up to 3 knowledge points for every neighbourly help chest while the effect is active).
Rating
 10/10
