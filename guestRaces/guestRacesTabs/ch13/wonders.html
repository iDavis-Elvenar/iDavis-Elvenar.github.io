..:: Amuni ::..
..:: Wonders ::..
The Chapter 13 introduces two new wonders: Pyramid of Purification and Lighthouse of Good Neighborhood. In this overview, we will take a look at their effects, avatars and I will share with you a couple of tips and tricks on how to get the most out of these wonders and some of my opinions about their future in-game prognosis.

..:: Pyramid of Purification ::..
Size
 4x6
Effects
 Produces a certain amount of Heavy Melee Units for your Barracks depending on your current Units per Squad
from 22.5% at level 1 to 172.5% at level 35
Gives a certain amount of boosted Sentient Goods of a random type when collecting Orcs (as a percentage of your Orc Production)
from 30% at level 1 to 200% at level 30
Tips & Evaluation
 To get the most out of this wonder, you will need to incorporate numerous armories into your city. However, I won't claim that this wonder alone is the reason for occupying additional space with armories, especially if you don't necessarily need them for orcs. In Chapter 13, my Sentient Goods production sufficed from Manufactories, particularly after I completed Chapter 14 and unlocked the Simia Sapiens wonder, which reduced its decay.
Nonetheless, when I adopted a new strategy in later chapters and added several armories into my city (to enhance the effects of the Temple of Spirits wonder and boost my ranking points (both thanks to the high amount of culture they consume)), I decided to construct the Pyramid of Purification retrospectively. After upgrading it to the highest levels, the amount of Sentient Goods I now receive daily from collecting orcs is more than enough to satisfy all the needs I have even without any additional production of Sentient Goods in manufactories. Therefore, I recommend to focus on primary sources of Sentient Goods first (such as leveling your manufactories and reducing its decay by the Simia Sapiens wonder) and only utilize the Pyramid of Purification once you have more compelling reasons to increase the amount of armories in your city and have discovered other resources that require your attention (such as Ascended Goods).
As for the second effect, while it is a nice addition, I don't consider it to be the primary reason for constructing this wonder.
Rating
 8/10

..:: Lighthouse of Good Neighborhood ::..
Size
 8x3
Effects
 Increases the duration of an "Ensorcelled Endowment" Enchantment
from 15% at level 1 to 106% at level 35
Increases the effect of an "Ensorcelled Endowment" Enchantment
from 6% at level 1 to 12% at level 31
Increases the Mana production on Neighborly Help-boosted Culture buildings
from 15% at level 1 to 115% at level 35
Tips & Evaluation
 The effect of this wonder is purely situational, as it depends on how much your Mana income relies on cultural buildings. If you tend to collect numerous Mana-producing buildings from events, and their output constitutes the majority of your Mana income, then it makes sense to build this wonder and upgrade it to high levels.
However, I have always preferred the Dragon Abbey for mana, as my active participation in Tournaments has resulted in thousands of spells in my inventory, which I can use whenever I need more Mana. I only own a few Mana-producing cultural buildings in my city, and I reduce their decay through the Maze of the Dark Matter ancient wonder.
Furthermore, I have never considered the effect of increasing the power of the Ensorcelled Endowment enchantment to be a compelling reason for constructing this wonder.
Rating
 6/10
