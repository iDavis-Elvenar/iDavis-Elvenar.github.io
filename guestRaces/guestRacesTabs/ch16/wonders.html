..:: Wonders ::..
The Chapter 16 introduces two new wonders: Spire Library and Tournament Arena. In this overview, we will take a look at their effects, avatars and I will share with you a couple of tips and tricks on how to get the most out of these wonders and some of my opinions about their future in-game prognosis.

..:: Spire Library ::..
Size
 4x6
Effects
 Gives extra Ranking Points per Fellowship reward (Spire Crystal) that you and your Fellowship unlocked at the end of the Spire of Eternity
from 0.5 ranking points per crystal at level 1 to 17.5 ranking points at level 35
Produces Knowledge Points every 24 hours
from 1 KP at level 1 to 7 KPs at level 31
Tips & Evaluation
 While I did choose to construct this wonder in my city, I gave higher priority to other wonders first. Upon careful calculation, it becomes evident that it would take years to recoup the Knowledge Points spent on upgrading this wonder solely from its daily effect. Hence, the only relevant effect for me was the extra Ranking Points per Spire Crystal. The effectiveness of this effect depends on how much value you place on your position in the Ranking and how proficiently you and your fellowship can achieve commendable results in the Spire of Eternity.
If you find both aspects important and advantageous, building this wonder might make sense. However, it primarily hinges on your personal preferences. It's worth noting that this wonder is not a must-have structure, and it won't provide any important benefits to your city.

 5/10

..:: Tournament Arena ::..
Size
 4x5
Effects
 Gives extra Ranking Points per Tournament Chest completed
from 0.5 ranking points per crystal at level 1 to 17.5 ranking points at level 35
Produces a certain amount of Venom Drone Riders every 3 hours, depending on your current Units per Squad
from 22.5% at level 1 to 172.5% at level 35
Tips & Evaluation
 Similarly to Spire Library, while I did choose to construct this wonder in my city, I gave higher priority to other wonders first. The production of Venom Drone Riders was not a decisive factor in determining whether I should build this wonder or not. Hence, the only relevant effect for me was the extra Ranking Points per Tournament Chest. The effectiveness of this effect depends on how much value you place on your position in the Ranking and how proficiently you and your fellowship can achieve commendable results in the Tournaments.
If you find both aspects important and advantageous, building this wonder might make sense. However, it primarily hinges on your personal preferences. It's worth noting that similarly to Spire Library, this wonder is not a must-have structure, and it won't provide any important benefits to your city.
