Intro
Hello and welcome to the All Guest Races - the brand-new subpage on my website! Here I will go through all different chapters that we have in the game and for each I will create a different content, covering their mechanics, strategies, ideal layouts, tips and much more! I will also add many of my own impressions for different chapters, so this subpage will also work as a sort of written guides for the game - which is a bit different approach that we had on the website so far.

Story Quests
This sounds like a long-run content and I will be, indeed, adding various guides over the long time periods.
My very first touch with this feature is the Quests Lists implementation, which is now fully working and is accessible from the panel on the left side of this site!
You can use the well known mechanics you are used to from Events subpage and get through all the story quests of the game! These lists include tasks, rewards and interactive tools for marking your quests as completed, as well as highlighting them for your further preparation.

How about the translations?
The All Guest Races subpage does not work with translation system of this website. For this reason, I can't use your selected language for all of the content included. However, you are free to use any tool that comes to your convenience and you might find it useful that the quest lines are now fully built for copy-paste. I will be considering further options to add this functionality in the future. All the feedback is of course welcomed and you can share it via the contact page of this website.
