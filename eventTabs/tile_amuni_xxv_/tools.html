..:: Tools ::..
The Chiming Sands event introduces a game mechanic that is completely unique in Elvenar! The Buried City has appeared right next to your city and now it's up to you to clear the dust, find your path and collect all the hidden treasures and artifacts that the Amuni event has to offer!
Shovel Bottled Wind Vacuum Vial
Clears the dust from one single tile. Can only be used on tiles covered by dust. Clears the dust from a column of fog tiles. Blocked by obstacles. Can only be used on cleared empty tiles. Clears the dust from all adjacent tiles. Can only be used on cleared empty tiles.
Each tool has a different area to clear and it's up to you to use these as efficiently as possible, so that you get the most out of your Provinsions. You can check the Strategies tab to read about well proven strategies of the Buried City.


In the left side of the event window you can see all your currently available tools. To buy more, you need to visit the Digging Tool Shop (using the + button under the Vacuum Vial icon).


I recommend you to always purchase the packs of bundled tools (by turns). You will receive the biggest number of tools per 1 provision spent.

Keep in mind that if you stick to the Strategies, the ratio of tools you use should be always approximately the same as the ratio of tools that is provided by the purchase of this style.
