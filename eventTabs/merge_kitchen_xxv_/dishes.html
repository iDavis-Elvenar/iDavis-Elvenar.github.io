..:: Dishes ::..
Dishes are the main target of the event! You need dishes to satisfy customers and complete their offers (you can find a detailed list of all offers in the tab Offers).

There are 3 types of dishes in total and each consists of 6 different levels (tiers). You can review all the available dishes in the table below:

Name of the dish Level 1 Level 2 Level 3 Level 4 Level 5 Level 6
Hot Chocolate
Lime Dessert
Delicious Roast
When you take a closer look at the Stockpiles tab, you can see how the dishes are spawned in your kitchen. Generally speaking, it is more likely that the spawning process itself will give you some of the low-level dishes and you will need to start the merging process to get the following tiers. The merging process is where the core of this mechanic lies!

In order to start the merging process, you need to have 2 dishes of the same type and the same level in your kitchen. By dragging one of these dishes and dropping onto the other, you merge them and create one dish of the following level (and the same type, of course).
By adding the low-level pieces to your board and repeating the merging process, you can receive the required pieces to fulfill your tasks. And what are the chances of generating the respective pieces? More about that in the Stockpiles part below.
..:: Stockpiles ::..
There are 3 stockpiles in total, each generating its own type of dishes. The Milk Stockpile generates Hot Chocolate, the Lime Pack generates Lime Dessert and the Vegetable Box generates Delicious Roast.
Drop Rate
Stockpile 40% 30% 15% 10% 5%
As we can see, each cup can generate different levels of respective pieces (with varying chances). It can happen that a cup will generate some of the higher level pieces than the one required in your tasks. Do not discard such pieces! Unless you are spawning them too much, you shouldn't get into excess of your pieces in the board and discarding would only mean losing your Dwarven Chips!
..:: Loot ::..
The Flavor Loot is a special item to be won among different customers' offers during the Mama Juul's Fusion Feast event! You can see the list of available customers' offers in the Offers tab and observe, which of these offers contain the Flavor Loot among prizes!

You can use this item similarly to the stockpiles and it will generate one random reward for you, among which you can find some high-level dishes and even additional event currency for your city! Take a look at the table below to see the detailed prizes of the Flavor Loot:
Flavor Loot 5% 5% 10% 10% 10% 15% 15% 15% 15%
75 100 1 1 1 1 1 1 50
This is the only possibility to gain additional event currency during this event. Unlike in previous events, the additional event currency cannot be obtained directly from opening chests, which brings some more variability into this event and allows us to also receive various items required for the actual gameplay in the kitchen (which is a very nice change in my opinion).
