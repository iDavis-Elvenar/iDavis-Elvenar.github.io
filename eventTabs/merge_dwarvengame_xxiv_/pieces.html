..:: Pieces ::..
Pieces are the main component of the event! You need them to complete the offers of dwarves (you can find a detailed list of such offers in the Trades tab).

There are 3 types of pieces in total and each comes with a set of 6 different levels (tiers). You can review all the available pieces in the table below:

Name of the piece Level 1 Level 2 Level 3 Level 4 Level 5 Level 6
Dwarven Figurines
Dwarven Tools
Dices
When you take a closer look at the Cups part below, you can follow more closely how the pieces are spawned on your board. Generally speaking, it is likely you receive some of the low-level pieces from the spawning process and you need to start the merging process to get the higher tiers. The merging process is where the core of this mechanic lies!

In order to start the merging process, you need to have 2 pieces of the same type and the same level on your board. By dragging one of these pieces and dropping onto the other, you merge them and create the piece of the same type but the following level.

By adding the low-level pieces to your board and repeating the merging process, you can receive the required pieces to fulfill your tasks. And what are the chances of generating the respective pieces? More about that in the Cups part below.
..:: Cups ::..
There are 3 cups in total, each generating its own type of pieces. The Yellow Cup generates the Dwarven Figurines, the Red Cup generates the Dwarven Tools and the Green Cup generates Dices.
Drop Rate
Cup 40% 30% 15% 10% 5%
As we can see, each cup can generate different levels of respective pieces (with varying chances). It can happen that a cup will generate some of the higher level pieces than the one required in your tasks. Do not discard such pieces! Unless you are spawning them too much, you shouldn't get into excess of your pieces in the board and discarding would only mean losing your Dwarven Chips!
..:: Cards ::..
The Lucky Draw (card) is a special item to be won among different dwarves' offers during the event! You can see the list of available dwarves' offers in the Trades tab and observe, which of these offers contain Lucky Draw card among prizes!

You can use this item similarly to the cups and it will generate one random reward for you, among which you can find some high-level pieces or even additional event currency! Take a look at the table below to see the detailed list of prizes in Lucky Draw:
Lucky Draw 5% 5% 10% 10% 10% 15% 15% 15% 15%
75 100 1 1 1 1 1 1 50
The Lucky Draw is the only possibility to obtain additional Event Currency in this event.
