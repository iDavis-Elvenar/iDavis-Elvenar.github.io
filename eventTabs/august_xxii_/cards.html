..:: Cards ::..
The Lucky Draw (card) is a special item to be won among different dwarven offers during the Gateway into the Past event! You can see the list of available dwarven offers in the Trades tab and observe, which of these offers contain Lucky Draw card among prizes!

You can use this item similarly to the cups and it will generate one random reward for you, among which you can find some high-level items or even additional event currency for your account. Take a look at the table below to see the detailed prizes of Lucky Draw:
Lucky Draw 5% 5% 5% 10% 10% 10% 10% 15% 15% 15%
3 50 75 1 1 1 25 1 1 1
This is the only possibility to gain additional event currency during this event. Unlike in previous events, where additional event currency could be obtained directly from opening chests, this event brings more variability and allows us to not only receive the currency, but also various items required for the actual gameplay in the board (which, in my opinion, is a nice change).
