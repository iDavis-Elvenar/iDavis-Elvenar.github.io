..:: Transcendence Buildings ::..
Transcendence Buildings is the newest feature in Elvenar introduced by the Magic of Yore event! Some of the evolving buildings will now offer special effects once you evolve them to certain stages. An example of such a building is the main building of this event, the Place of Convergence:

When examining the evolution stages of these buildings, you will notice a special symbol at some of their final stages:

This indicates that the transcendence effects become available once you upgrade the building to this stage. Transcendence effects are temporary abilities provided by the building, which you can activate using a new resource called Volatile Sigils . Some of these resources can already be collected through the Grand Prizes in this event (check the Prizes tab), and additional ways to obtain them will be introduced in the future.
You can extend the duration of the Transcendence effect at any time by using additional Volatile Sigils. However, each subsequent activation requires an increasing number of Volatile Sigils.

To find more details about the new Place of Convergence building, visit the All Buildings page.
