..:: Strategies ::..
The Misty Forest is my favorite event type as it is the only mechanic that allows players to fully pull out their strategic skills. Unlike rotating chests or shuffle board events, the tile event gives you much more responsibility on affecting the rewards and progress you receive (which in my opinion makes event much more interesting after all).

In the Tools tab we have described all the tools you have at your disposal. Each of the tools has its own special effect and it's up to you to use them as efficiently as possible! I'm going to describe several well proven strategies that may help you on your way to maximizing your rewards. (The content will be added soon).
