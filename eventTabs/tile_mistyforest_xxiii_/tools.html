..:: Tools ::..
The Whispers of the Soul event introduces a game mechanic that is completely unique in Elvenar! The Misty Forest has appeared right next to your city and now it's up to you to clear the fog, find your path and collect all the hidden spirits and mysterious rewards that the Halloween event has to offer!
Candle Lantern Flash Flask
Clears the fog from one single tile. Can only be used on tiles covered by fog. Clears the fog from a column of fog tiles. Blocked by obstacles. Can only be used on cleared empty tiles. Clears the fog from all adjacent tiles. Can only be used on cleared empty tiles.
Each tool has a different area to clear and it's up to you to use these as efficiently as possible, so that you get the most out of your Magic Ingredients. You can check the Strategies tab to read about well proven strategies of the Misty Forest.


In the left side of the event window you can see all your currently available tools. To buy more, you need to visit the Magical Lights Shop (using the + button under the Flash Flask icon).


I recommend you to always purchase the packs of bundled tools (by turns). You will receive the biggest number of tools per 1 ingredient spent.

Keep in mind that if you stick to the Strategies, the ratio of tools you use should be always approximately the same as the ratio of tools that is provided by the purchase of this style.
