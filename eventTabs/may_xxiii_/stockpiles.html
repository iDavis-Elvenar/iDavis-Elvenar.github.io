There are 3 stockpiles in total, each generating its own type of dishes. The __ generates the Hot Chocolate, the __ generates the Lime Dessert and the __ generates Delicious Roast.

Cup 40% 30% 15% 10% 5%
We know that each stockpile can generate even better dish than level 1 (with a decreasing chance). In case the spawning process generates a better dish than the one needed in our task, it may seem like we have received a potentially useless dish for now (which could lead us to think that the worst-case scenario numbers calculated in the Dishes tab may get even worse), but we will certainly find a use for this piece in one of the very following offers and that will reduce our costs markedly. So do not discard your high level dishes! Unless you are clicking the stockpile buttons too many times (and more than the number of level 1 dishes you need), you shouldn't get into excess of your dishes in the kitchen!

Considering the merging process from the Dishes tab and spawning process from the table above, we can visualize the whole gameplay mechanic. Use the Play button to start the visualization and click the respective Merge nodes to merge your generated pieces (if you already have some, of course).
You can also use the Step button to have better control over the merging process.
