..:: Loots ::..
The Flavor Loot is a special item to be won among different customers' offers during the Mama Juul's Fusion Feast event! You can see the list of available customers' offers in the Offers tab and observe, which of these offers contain the Flavor Loot among prizes!

You can use this item similarly to the stockpiles and it will generate one random reward for you, among which you can find some high-level dishes and even additional event currency for your city! Take a look at the table below to see the detailed prizes of the Flavor Loot:
Flavor Loot 5% 5% 10% 10% 10% 15% 15% 15% 15%
75 100 1 1 1 1 1 1 50
This is the only possibility to gain additional event currency during this event. Unlike in previous events, the additional event currency cannot be obtained directly from opening chests, which brings some more variability into this event and allows us to also receive various items required for the actual gameplay in the kitchen (which is a very nice change in my opinion).
