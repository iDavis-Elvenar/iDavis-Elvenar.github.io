..:: Community Knowledge ::..

The Community Knowledge is the latest feature on my website, related to the closure of the Community Forums announced on September 11th, 2023. InnoGames has decided to close the Community Forums later this year and shift the focus to the newly created server on Discord. From now on, players can use the official Elvenar server on Discord to discuss the game.

The unfortunate news is that this change will make us lose a wealth of interesting information that has been created over the years - once the Community Forums are closed and no longer accessible. That's why I'm introducing to you the Community Knowledge - an archive of all the information from the forums that is either important, interesting or considered nostalgic by you or the community!
..:: Phase 1 - Data Collection ::..
This project wouldn't be possible without your support! The community is spread across numerous countries and nearly 20 different languages. As you are coming from your own community, you know the best what the interesting content in your forums is!

Are you aware of any interesting and popular topics in your forum? Do you find particular content important to future players? Is there a specific section or area on your forum that you keep returning to? Or do you simply want to have some content stored because it holds your memories and you feel nostalgic about it?

This is your moment! The forums will be closed at the end of this year, and I've prepared an automatic way to gather data from the most popular topics to be saved here on my website! You will always be able to return to your favorite content even after the forums are permanently closed!

Take a look at the current sample of the Community Knowledge to get an idea of what the final version will look like! The sample version currently only includes a few topics.

..:: How can I help? ::..
It's simple! All you have to do is visit your forum (no matter what nationality you are - all languages are welcomed!) and send me as many links to your favorite topics as you wish - whether it's 3, 5, 10, 20 or more! You can even send the links to the entire sections if you feel like they are worth it!

Visit the form here!

The Phase 1 will be ended on December 12th, 2023. I will then take my time to process all the content you - as a community - have sent me and will scrape the forums to store their content here on my website! You can already start sharing the topics with me, to make sure you reserve enough time for this process! Once the forums are closed, no more content can be archived! ⏳
..:: What are the limitations? ::..
You may be wondering - how much content can my website store? Well, I obviously can't store the entire forums. However, I've reserved ample capacity for this project, so you don't have to worry too much about space. Still, it's not possible to save every single topic, so it's important to prioritize the content you share with me.

..:: Why is the Community Knowledge read-only? ::..
If you're wondering why I haven't created a fully functional forum for you to respond to and interact with, you're absolutely right. The technical capabilities of my website, with a few adjustments, could easily support the creation of a fully functional forum, serving as a replacement for the forums many of you were accustomed to. However, following InnoGames' decision, the official means of communication are now shifting to Discord. This will be the primary platform for all future official communications, including contests, feedback, and much more.

The increasing popularity of my website over the past months, combined with the introduction of a new forum, could potentially create a significant barrier between InnoGames and their goals of establishing an official social media platform. As a content creator, I want to avoid interfering with their official plans and avoid competing with them. I have immense respect for the players who cherished our official forums, and I've chosen to take a more delicate approach by providing a repository of memories for something that we won't be able to replicate in the future.

This decision aims to align with InnoGames' vision and ensure that players have a seamless and unified experience on Discord, which has been chosen as the official communication platform going forward.

I eagerly anticipate your responses and am thrilled to embark on an exciting new project for my website with all of you!
