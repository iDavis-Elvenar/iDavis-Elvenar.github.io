Reach me on Discord


I have created a space on Discord, where you can reach me out more effectively and provide your feedback more easily.

1) I am not yet on Discord
If you are not yet on Discord and want to join, you can use the invitation link HERE. After selecting your nickname, setting your date of birth, and agreeing to Discord's Terms of Service, you will be introduced to the Welcome Center of the official Elvenar server. To access the iDavis-Elvenar website-related space, make sure to select "English" as one of your languages. This will give you access to the English section of the server, where my dedicated space is located.

Discord is a free and safe communication platform for gaming communities, where users can chat and share information about the game. It is very popular today and joining it will definitely enhance your gaming experience!

2) I am already on the server
Once you have joined the Discord server, congratulations! In the list of channels on the left-hand side, you can find the English section. One of the channels there is called "elvenar-discussions" - click it to open.


Next, among the threads, search for "iDavis-Elvenar: Updates & Feedback".


This is the thread you can use to provide feedback and chat with me in real time.

I hope you find this useful. This new method will now be preferred over the Contact Form via email, which you can still find below.
