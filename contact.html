About Me

I would like to use this space to share some information about me, both as a player and as a person. Not sure how many people are actually interested in any side information, so I will also add something about my YouTube channel and this website.

My name is David, I come from Slovakia, its beautiful capital city Bratislava and I'm a computer science student.

This website was created as my hobby, free-time project, originally coming from the idea of creating a very first interactive database of all buildings from the game Elvenar, published by InnoGames. Since its initial launch in March 2021 I keep updating the website, for both - updating the database with the newest buildings from all the introduced events and updating the existing code for adding new features and additional improvements.

You may also know my YouTube channel - iDavis. I started developing this channel back in February 2019, with the initial idea of sharing a gameplay video from the event at that time. Over time the creation of my videos went across many different topics and ideas. Currently, I have several brand new ideas for the upcoming videos, that will also expand my portfolio with another game by InnoGames.
